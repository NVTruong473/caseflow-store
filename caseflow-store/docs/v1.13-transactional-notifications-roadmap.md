# v1.13 - Transactional Notifications And Simulated Transfer Roadmap

Date: 2026-07-22

## Objective

Add durable customer notifications, provider-ready email/SMS delivery,
optional phone verification, and staff-confirmed simulated bank-transfer
operations without introducing real payment settlement.

## Non-Goals

- No real bank account, QR deep link, payment gateway, settlement, or refund.
- No carrier, warehouse, or live map integration.
- No marketing campaigns or promotional SMS.
- No customer-visible sandbox controls or fake delivery claims.
- No mandatory phone verification until a live SMS provider is configured.

## Tasks

### NOTIFY-T01 - Accept ADR, Threat Model, And Roadmap

Acceptance criteria:

- Notification channels, provider modes, commerce isolation, and production
  locks are documented.
- Live-provider prerequisites and honest limitations are explicit.

Verification:

- ADR is indexed and roadmap exists.

### NOTIFY-T02 - Define Notification, OTP, And Provider Contracts

Acceptance criteria:

- Domain events, statuses, channels, templates, DTOs, and Zod schemas exist.
- Email/SMS provider interfaces expose no framework or UI dependency.
- Environment validation fails closed for incomplete live configuration.

Verification:

- TypeScript, lint, and focused contract tests pass.

### NOTIFY-T03 - Add Additive Supabase Notification Migration

Acceptance criteria:

- Outbox, in-app notification, and OTP challenge tables are additive.
- Unique idempotency keys, indexes, constraints, grants, and RLS are present.
- Order insert/status triggers enqueue deterministic events.
- Existing order, voucher, payment, and catalog rows are unchanged.

Verification:

- Migration contract verifier and count-preserving preflight pass.

### NOTIFY-T04 - Implement Providers And Dispatcher

Acceptance criteria:

- In-app delivery is always available.
- Sandbox email/SMS writes staff-only preview metadata and never calls a
  network provider.
- Live email and SMS adapters use server-only environment values and fail
  closed when incomplete.
- Retry, exponential backoff, terminal failure, and idempotency are enforced.

Verification:

- Provider, dispatcher, duplicate-event, timeout, and secret-leakage tests pass.

### NOTIFY-T05 - Add Customer Verification And Notification UI

Acceptance criteria:

- Account page shows verified email/phone state and an account-scoped inbox.
- Phone OTP request/verify APIs are rate-limited, hashed, expiring, and
  disabled for customers when no live SMS provider exists.
- Customer notification read actions cannot cross account boundaries.

Verification:

- Customer auth, inbox, OTP edge cases, mobile layout, and accessibility pass.

### NOTIFY-T06 - Integrate Order Events And Simulated Transfer Operations

Acceptance criteria:

- Order-created, payment-confirmed, order-confirmed, cancelled, shipping, and
  completed transitions enqueue idempotent notifications.
- Staff/admin can confirm or reject simulated bank transfer using protected
  server transitions.
- Customers cannot self-confirm payment in production.

Verification:

- Checkout, customer cancellation, staff/admin transitions, duplicate events,
  and production payment lock pass.

### NOTIFY-T07 - Add Admin/Staff Notification Operations

Acceptance criteria:

- Operations UI lists channel, event, recipient-safe label, status, attempts,

- Staff can inspect/retry operational failures; provider configuration remains

- No secret, raw OTP, full provider response, or unnecessary PII is rendered.

Verification:

- Role matrix, filters, retry, empty/error/loading state, and CSV/privacy gates
