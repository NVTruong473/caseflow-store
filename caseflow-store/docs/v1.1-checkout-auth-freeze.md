# CaseFlow Books Checkout/Auth Freeze

Status: accepted by `D34-T03` on 2026-07-16

## Scope

This freeze closes the `v1.1` customer-auth, checkout, payment simulation,
customer order history, and public order tracking work before Day 35 admin
operations expand.

Frozen behavior:

- Anonymous visitors can browse, add books to the local cart, and visit account
  pages, but `/checkout` redirects to `/account?next=/checkout`.
- Checkout completion requires an authenticated `customer` role.
- Customer checkout requires full name, email, phone, and default Vietnam
  shipping address.
- The server recalculates catalog, stock, shipping, VAT estimate, payment fee,
  display FX estimate, and total before creating an order.
- VND remains the authoritative persisted currency.
- Payment methods are simulated COD, bank transfer, MoMo, ZaloPay, and VNPay.
- No card number, CVV, expiry, real wallet credential, bank credential, or
  provider login detail is collected.
- Customers can view their own order history at `/account/orders`.
- Public order tracking at `/orders/track` requires order code plus matching
  checkout email or phone.
- Public order tracking responses do not expose customer email, phone, shipping
  address, or item detail.

## Allowed Changes After Freeze

- Release-blocking bug fixes.
- Test, documentation, and screenshot updates.
- Admin/staff operations from Day 35 onward, as long as they do not weaken the
  customer checkout/auth contracts above.
- Explicitly approved roadmap or ADR changes.

## Changes Requiring Review

- Allowing guest checkout for `v1.1`.
- Adding real payment providers or real provider redirects.
- Collecting real payment, wallet, bank, or provider credentials.
- Treating phone numbers as verified without a real OTP/SMS provider.
- Changing server-owned total calculation or trusting browser totals.
- Returning customer contact/address/item detail from public tracking.
- Making order code alone sufficient for public tracking.
- Changing role authorization boundaries for customer, staff, or admin access.

## Remaining Risks

- Payment is still simulated. No real provider, webhook verification,
  settlement, refunds, disputes, or reconciliation exists.
- Phone numbers are required but not verified by SMS/OTP.
- Email verification depends on Supabase Auth configuration and was previously
  rate-limited during QA.
- Public order tracking has guarded lookup semantics, but no production rate
  limiter, CAPTCHA, or abuse monitoring yet.
- Customer order notifications by email/SMS are not implemented.
- Cart remains local to one browser and is not account-synced.
- Stock is checked before order creation but not reserved or decremented yet.
- VAT, FX, and international payment fees are configurable estimates, not legal
  or tax advice.
- Admin/staff operations still need least-privilege permissions, audit trails,
  and operational controls in later Day 35-38 tasks.

## Freeze Decision

Checkout/auth may proceed into Day 35 admin operations because the customer
journey has account gating, server-owned totals, payment-state simulation, own
order history, guarded public tracking, and explicit limitation documentation.
The remaining risks are accepted for the portfolio `v1.1` scope and must not be
presented as solved production-commerce capabilities.
