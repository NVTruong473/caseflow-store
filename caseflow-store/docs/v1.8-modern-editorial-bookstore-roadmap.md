## Explicitly Rejected For v1.8

- Reviews, ratings, sales counts, and bestseller claims without real data.
- Newsletter without email consent/storage/provider behavior.
- Blog/editorial articles without real content source.
- Wishlist/recently viewed without storage and privacy decisions.
- Quick-view modal until focus-trap, variant selection, and cart behavior are
  scoped separately.
- Official cover replacement without a verified rights/metadata pipeline.
- Heavy animation or scroll hijacking.
