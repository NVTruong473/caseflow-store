# Entry 30 - CV And Interview Summary

Type: retrospective note, not an additional development day.

## Strongest CV Story

CaseFlow Store is a deployed full-stack e-commerce MVP built with Next.js, TypeScript, Supabase, Zod, and Playwright. It includes a storefront, product listing and detail pages, local cart, server-validated checkout, Supabase order persistence, admin authentication, protected order management, responsive UI, stable API errors, and production deployment.

## Evidence To Mention

- Production URL: `https://caseflow-store.vercel.app`.
- Release tag: `v1.0.0`.
- Local Playwright: 20/20 passed before release.
- Production Playwright: 20/20 passed before release.
- Catalog: 5 categories and 16 products.
- Security: server-side price recalculation, RLS, role checks, no committed secrets.
- Docs: README, architecture, ADR index, known limitations, CV bullets, and journal entries.

## Claims To Avoid

Do not claim real payment processing, real shipping integration, marketplace scale, high traffic, advanced analytics, or production business revenue. The project is a verified portfolio MVP, not a live commercial operation.

## Next Interview Angle

The best interview angle is pragmatic engineering: controlled scope, evidence-based gates, secure server-side validation, production deployment, and honest limitations.
