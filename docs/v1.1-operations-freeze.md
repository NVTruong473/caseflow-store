# CaseFlow Books Operations Freeze

Status: accepted by `D38-T03` on 2026-07-17

## Scope

This freeze closes the `v1.1` admin and staff operations phase before Day 39
assistant, SEO, accessibility, and performance work begins.

Frozen behavior:

- Admin and staff share the operations workspace shell and dashboard.
- Staff can use day-to-day operations: dashboard, order list and operations,
  catalog management, inventory adjustment, and customer lookup.
- Admin can use every staff workflow plus promotion management and the
  admin-only settings boundary.
- Every admin/staff API checks role and permission server-side; UI visibility is
  not an authorization boundary.
- Customers receive `FORBIDDEN` and anonymous visitors receive `UNAUTHORIZED`
  for admin/staff APIs.
- Catalog management can create and update sellable book editions without
  changing the frozen book domain/schema contract.
- Inventory adjustment writes stock changes and adjustment rows through the
  server-side workflow.
- Promotion management is admin-only because discount rules affect revenue and
  checkout totals.
- Order operations support server-backed filters, order status, payment status,
  shipping status, and internal notes with server-side transition checks.
- The sales and inventory dashboard shows server-calculated order, payment,
  inventory-risk, top-book, and recent-order metrics.
- Order CSV export is server-generated, protected by `orders:read`, and excludes
  customer email, phone, full shipping address, customer id, and internal notes.

## Allowed Changes After Freeze

- Release-blocking bug fixes.
- Security fixes that preserve or strengthen server-side authorization.
- Test, documentation, screenshot, and portfolio evidence updates.
- Day 39 assistant, SEO, accessibility, and performance work, as long as it does
  not weaken operations permissions, data contracts, or checkout totals.
- Day 40 release candidate, deployment, smoke-test, and documentation work.
- Explicitly approved roadmap or ADR changes.

## Changes Requiring Review

- Adding new operations features or changing admin/staff responsibilities.
- Changing staff/admin permission mapping.
- Making promotion management available to staff.
- Exporting customer PII, full addresses, customer ids, or internal notes in
  CSV or public/customer APIs.
- Relaxing server-side order, payment, or shipping transition checks.
- Trusting browser-provided role, permission, price, stock, subtotal, tax, fee,
  promotion, or total values.
- Changing the frozen book schema, domain enums, or public catalog API contract.
- Claiming real payment, shipping, settlement, audit, or analytics capability
  without provider integration and tests.

## Remaining Risks

- Payment and shipping remain simulated. There is no provider webhook,
  settlement, refund, dispute, carrier label, or tracking integration.
- Inventory is adjustable, but order creation still does not reserve or
  decrement stock automatically.
- Internal notes exist, but there is no immutable staff action audit trail yet.
- CSV export is acceptable for portfolio scale; a large production dataset would
  need pagination, background jobs, or signed expiring exports.
- No production rate limiter, CAPTCHA, or abuse monitoring exists for admin
  login or public order tracking.
- Dashboard revenue remains an estimate, not accounting-grade reporting.
- Role management UI is still a boundary placeholder; concrete role assignment
  controls are not implemented.

## Freeze Decision

Operations may proceed into Day 39 because the admin/staff workflows, dashboard,
CSV export, permission boundaries, and cleanup evidence are verified for the
portfolio `v1.1` scope. Future work should improve quality around this frozen
surface instead of adding more operations features without review.
