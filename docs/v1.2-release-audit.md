## Scope

`v1.2.0` releases the Realistic Bookstore Content & Merchandising Upgrade.
The release replaces placeholder-driven bookstore presentation with a
provenance-first, 100-edition catalog; project-created cover artwork;
truthful merchandising shelves; source/content-quality checks; upgraded
storefront discovery; and bounded admin merchandising operations.

This is still a focused bookstore and small-business operations demo. It is
not a marketplace, payment processor, licensed commercial metadata feed, or
shipping-carrier integration.

## Accepted Non-Blockers

- Checkout records orders but does not process real COD, bank, MoMo, ZaloPay,
  VNPay, or card payments.
- Customer phone/email readiness is enforced by profile data, not by real
  SMS/OTP or email verification.
- Shipping, VAT, FX, and international payment-fee values are configurable
  estimates, not legal/tax advice or live carrier/bank quotes.
- Stock is decremented at order creation, but there is no separate reservation
  or carrier fulfillment workflow.
- Public order tracking is guarded by access code, but does not yet include a
  dedicated rate limiter beyond the app/Supabase/Vercel platform boundaries.
- The catalog uses factual source-reviewed metadata with self-written display
  copy and project-created SVG cover artwork; it does not use licensed
  commercial cover images or a commercial book metadata feed.
- Admin content operations are intentionally bounded to quality/source signals,
  allowed project-written content fixes, and approved shelf active/sort state.
