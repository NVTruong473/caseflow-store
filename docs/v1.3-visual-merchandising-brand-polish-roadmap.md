# CaseFlow Books v1.3 Visual Merchandising And Brand Polish Roadmap

- Status: Completed locally; not deployed or tagged
- Date: 2026-07-18
- Governing ADR: `ADR-0008`
- Scope: post-`v1.2.0` visual merchandising and brand polish

## Goal

Make CaseFlow Books feel visually richer, more bookstore-specific, and less
template-like while preserving the released e-commerce flows, server trust
boundaries, catalog provenance, and admin operations.

## Non-Goals

- No real payment provider.
- No SMS/OTP or real email verification.
- No shipping-carrier integration.
- No database schema migration.
- No commercial cover copying, external stock imagery, fake ratings, fake
  sold counts, or bestseller claims.
- No full rebuild of the route tree.
